SAMRIDH Accelerator Program

CIIC Accelerator Programs:

CIIC provide intensive and time-limited business support for cohorts of startups, aiming to get them ready for investment more quickly than traditional incubators.

Overview
  • CIIC support early-stage, growth-driven companies through education, mentorship, and financing. Startups enter CIIC for a fixed-period of time, and as part of a cohort. The CIIC accelerator experience is a process of intense, rapid, and immersive education aimed at accelerating the life cycle of young innovative companies, compressing years’ worth of learning-by-doing into just a few months.
  • Startups need support, coaching and education about changes in the market, process and technology, along with guidance on industry best practices. Startups also require funding, mentoring, office space and equipment, as well as a skilled workforce.
  • >CIIC bridge these gaps by providing startups with market access, high-quality mentorship, networking opportunities, talent, co-working spaces, and professional services. Beyond that, CIIC connect startups with high-profile mentors and investors to help them overcome these obstacles.

MeitY SAMRIDH accelerator program:

01

In order to promote and empower startup ventures and boost entrepreneurship, economic growth, and employment across the country the government ministry of electronics and information technology launched a programme Start-up Accelerators of MeitY for product Innovation, Development and growth (SAMRIDH)” by MeitY startup Hub (MSH) which aims to boost start-up ecosystem in country.

02

The scheme will provide support to selected startups with equity funding upto Rs. 80 lakhs [maximum 40 lakhs from Meity and Rs 40 lakhs and above from a co-investor].

03

The scheme heralds a very important milestone in our journey towards building confidence in our startup community.
